### A Song for Cub Scouts

#### Tune: The Farmer in the Dell
> Our honored guests are here  
> Our honored guests are here  
> Stand up now and take a bow  
>  
> Our honored guests are here  
>  
> Let's wave and say "Ni Hao" _(nee how="hello")_  
> Let's wave and say "Ni Hao"  
> Let's say hello to all our friends
