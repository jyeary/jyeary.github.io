The general equation for the volume of a cylindrical object is noted by
the following equation.

$$ v = \pi r^2h $$

![](/assets/images/volume.gif)

If a pizza has a radius of **z** and a height of **a**, then the volume
of a pizza can be determined by the following equation:

$$ p_v = pi * z * z * a $$
